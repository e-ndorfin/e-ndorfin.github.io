Built for Rift Rewind Hackathon with a team of 4. An AI-powered League of Legends coaching platform that analyzes player gameplay to provide personalized champion recommendations and actionable feedback.

**Key Features:**
- Champion recommender based on mastery data from thousands of players
- Ability similarity calculator comparing 600+ abilities across 200,000+ pairwise comparisons  
- In-depth match analysis with performance metrics (EPS, Gold Efficiency, Power Rankings)
- Heimer AI - personalized coach providing context-aware insights

**Tech Stack:** Python/FastAPI, React/TypeScript, Amazon Bedrock/SageMaker, Supabase, Riot Games API
